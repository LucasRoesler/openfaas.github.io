Support OpenFaaS

OpenFaaS is licensed as MIT and completely Open Source and free to use. However the amount of effort needed to maintain and develop new features is not sustainable without proper financial backing. You can support OpenFaaS through the following methods.

  • One-time donations
    You can make a one-time donation through PayPal
  • Patreon
    Pledges to Patreon go directly to support the work of Alex Ellis, you can get a unique reward, become a backer and see the latest funding goals.
  • OpenCollective
    Pledges made through OpenCollective are transparent and accumulate to offset expenses for community events and costs.
